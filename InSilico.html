In Silico Disease Modeling

Live Webinar

Wednesday, 10 June 2026

Online


Attend this webinar to gain insight into:

Disease Mechanism Modeling

Computational modeling approaches for understanding biological and pathological disease mechanisms.

Disease Progression Modeling

Modeling and prediction of disease dynamics, progression trajectories, and temporal changes.

Multiscale Modeling of Disease Processes

Computational modeling of disease processes across molecular, cellular, tissue, and organ scales.

Systems Modeling of Disease Interactions

Computational modeling of interactions, dependencies, and network structures within complex disease systems.

Schedule & Details

Date & Time Wednesday, 10 June 2026, 10:00–14:00 CEST (UTC+2)
Duration Approx. 4 hours
Platform Google Meet
Recording Available after the event

Webinar Access:
The webinar will take place via Google Meet and can be accessed directly in a browser on desktop, tablet, or smartphone without any additional installation. A microphone and webcam may be used, but are not required. Questions may also be asked via the chat.
